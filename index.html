Case Study

The compliance gap
no one else caught.

iSCAN is TransUnion CIBIL's platform for current account opening and maintenance — handling KYC and liability-side onboarding across web, API, and batch channels for member banks.

During a product review, it was identified that iSCAN's current account opening and maintenance flows were not adhering to applicable RBI norms — a gap that had persisted undetected through multiple product and tech review cycles.

The issue was flagged and corrected before any audit exposure. This is exactly the kind of compliance gap that sits at the boundary of product design, regulatory interpretation, and technical implementation — and is invisible to teams that don't hold all three simultaneously.

Compliance gap identified and remediated before external audit — zero regulatory exposure.
